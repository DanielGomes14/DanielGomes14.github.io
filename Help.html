Frequently Asked Questions


How do I go back to the previous page?

If there is an arrow pointing back or cross at the top bar you may click those, if there isn't you may access any page through the menu on the top left corner of your screen.


How do I access my Agenda?

Through the menu accessible by clicking the icon on the top left corner of your screen.


How add friends to an already existing project?

Access project settings by clicking the icon on the top right corner of project page and the second option will allow you to either add or remove members from that project.
